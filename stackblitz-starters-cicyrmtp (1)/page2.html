About Us

We started with a simple frustration: creativity shouldn’t be expensive. Our team has always loved sketching ideas—on napkins, notebooks, whiteboards, anything within reach. But when it came to digital tools, the barrier was clear. High-quality sketchpad devices were often overpriced, complicated, and out of reach for students, hobbyists, and everyday thinkers who just wanted a simple way to create. So we decided to build something different. Our company was founded on the belief that ideas deserve better tools—tools that are intuitive, reliable, and most importantly, accessible. We set out to design sketchpad devices that strip away unnecessary complexity and focus on what really matters: getting your ideas out of your head and onto the screen as naturally as possible. But the bigger challenge wasn’t just building a great product—it was making it affordable. From the beginning, we made a deliberate choice to keep our prices low. That meant rethinking everything: smarter design, efficient manufacturing, and cutting out the extras that don’t add real value. We didn’t want premium creativity to be limited to a small group of people. We wanted students, designers, engineers, and curious minds everywhere to have the same opportunity to create. Because innovation doesn’t come from expensive tools—it comes from people. Today, our mission remains the same: to make digital sketching simple, accessible, and available to anyone with an idea worth drawing.
